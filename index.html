Why Juliet?

Named after someone who gave
everything without counting the cost.

In Shakespeare's tragedy, Juliet keeps no accounts — she gives freely. This app is named after her as a quiet reminder: money, like love, should be intentional. Every rupee logged is a small act of self-awareness.
